Air Fryer Chicken Fajitas

It’s one of those delicious dinner ideas that combines chicken and vegetables for an all-around healthier and tasty meal. You can even warm the tortillas in the air fryer too.

If you haven’t tried air fried fajitas for dinner, start with this recipe. It gives you that juicy and crispy chicken paired with peppers and onions.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • bell pepper
  • red onion
  • oil
  • chili powder
  • cumin
  • lime juice
  • salt and pepper
  • cayenne

Directions

  1. Preheat air fryer to 370 degrees.
  2. Mix the chicken strips, bell pepper, red onion, oil, chili powder, cumin, lime juice, salt and pepper, and cayenne pepper (if using) in a bowl.
  3. Put the chicken fajitas in the air fryer and air fry for 10-13 minutes, until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees F at its thickest point.
  4. Remove the fajitas from the air fryer and enjoy with tortillas or on its own.
